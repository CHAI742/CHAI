Our Story

CHAI started as a student-led effort to bridge gaps in basic care for the homeless community.

We noticed a simple reality: minor wounds and unmanaged chronic conditions often spiral into emergencies. By supplying basic first‑aid and screenings, we prevent small issues from becoming crises.

Values

  • Dignity first: Every interaction is consent‑based and person‑centered.
  • Do the simple things well: Bandage, listen, connect.
  • Partnerships over pride: We collaborate with shelters and pharmacies.

Governance

CHAI is a Texas nonprofit pursuing 501(c)(3) status. We keep admin overhead lean and publish simple impact reports twice a year.
