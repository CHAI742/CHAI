Projects

Detailed breakdowns, budgets, and checklists you can reuse.

First‑Aid Kits

Contents: assorted bandages, antiseptic wipes, gauze, medical tape, nitrile gloves, ointment, heat/cold packs, alcohol pads.

Unit cost (target): $7.80

  • Supplies: $6.60
  • Bag & insert card: $0.70
  • Logistics & loss: $0.50
Tip: Ask local pharmacies to donate near‑expiry kits. Many will say yes if pickup is easy.

Health Screenings

Blood pressure and glucose checks during monthly outreach events.

  • Equipment: cuffs, glucometers, lancets, sharps container, disinfectant.
  • Roles: intake, screener, scribe, runner, supplies lead.
  • Data: only aggregate counts; no personal health info stored.

Referrals & Navigation

We use a one‑page resource card with QR codes to shelters, clinics, and crisis lines. Volunteers carry extras to hand out.
